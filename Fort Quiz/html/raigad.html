Raigad Fort Information

Raigad is a hill fort in the Raigad district that is about 25 kilometers from Mahad. In 1674 AD, Chhatrapati Shivaji made this fort his capital and renovated it. Mahad, in the Raigad District of Maharashtra, is home to the impressive and well-known Raigad Fort, which was built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It is refer to as the “Gibraltar of the East” all over the world because it is one of the strongest fortresses ever built on the Deccan Plateau. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the architect behind a number of Raigad structures and other constructions. After being crown king of the entire Maratha Kingdom and, later, the Maratha Empire, which encompass a significant portion of the central and western regions of India, he establish this as his capital in 1674.
